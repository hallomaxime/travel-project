Why Copenhagen?

Discover the charms of Copenhagen

Immerse yourself in the fairy-tale-like ambiance of Nyhavn, where colorful buildings line the waterfront and historic ships bob in the gentle waves. Wander through the cobbled streets of the Latin Quarter, where cozy cafes and trendy boutiques await around every corner.

For those seeking adventure, Copenhagen offers ample opportunities to explore the great outdoors. Rent a bike and pedal along the city's extensive network of cycle paths, or venture beyond the city limits to discover charming coastal villages, verdant forests, and pristine beaches.

Whether you're captivated by history, intrigued by design, or simply seeking a new and exciting destination to explore, Copenhagen promises an unforgettable experience that will leave you enchanted and inspired.

Life outside

The best parks in and around Copenhagen

Frederiksberg Have

For centuries, Frederiksberg Have has been a favorite among Copenhageners. Many bring their picnic baskets for lunch and top off the day by peeking into the Copenhagen Zoo in the north end of the gardens.

Address:

Frederiksberg Runddel
2000 Frederiksberg

Learn more

Kongens Have

Also known as Rosenborg Have, the beautiful King's Garden in central Copenhagen is the country's oldest royal gardens. Right beside Rosenborg Palace, its lovely lawns are a popular spot for a picnic in the summer.

Address:

Øster Voldgade 4
1350 København K

Learn more

Botanisk Have

The Botanisk Have is Denmark's largest collection of living plants, a few steps from Nørreport Station. It's a free public green space to roam and relax in, filled with a wide variety of rare trees, shrubs and herbs.

Address:

Gothersgade 128
1353 København K
